About Us

Edumania provides students with quality materials to study for their whole years of study. We offer both primary level, secondary level, and high school level courses for both science, arts, and business combinations. We also provide free guide for students through the use of our AI chatbot as well as teachers who volunteered for the development of education in Tanzania.
